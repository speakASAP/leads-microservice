# Goal 9 Context Package

## Source Context

Goal 5 states that future AI/CRM payloads must omit contact values, raw messages, confirmation tokens, private URLs, and raw metadata values. No AI or CRM client currently exists in Leads source.

## Non-Goals

- No AI microservice call.
- No CRM client.
- No raw lead export.
- No approval template or kill switch work.
- No production lead reads or mutations.
